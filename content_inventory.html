Site Goals
The client wishes for the site to bring in new clients. They want the site to explain their tours, what customers will seeing, and how long the tours are. The site's goal is to also schedule reservations.
Basic Demographic Information
The intended audience is visitors to Portland, Oregon. The customers want to explore the history of the city.
Deeper dive into the site goal
The client wants their site to look professional. They want the colors and fonts optimized for all screen sizes. The clients do not know what search terms to include in their site.
Call to action
The client wants visitors to Portland to choose their company over the competition. They have more experience than rival businesses and provide a more complete experience. They want site visitors to use the site to book tours.
Competitive analysis
The first site that was similar was TravelPortland.com. This site offered a local event calendar, a free guide book, and a App for visitors to download. These functions can incorporate with a focus on the history of Portland.
The second site was Brewvana.com. This site features brewery tours. They also sell merchandise. The client also offers these kind of tours. The client can improve their effectiveness by offering merchandise featuring local beers. The client can also offer Portland themed products like t-shirts and cups.
